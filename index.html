AI in DevOps (AIOps & MLOps)

Modern systems generate massive logs and metrics. Artificial Intelligence helps DevOps teams automate operations, predict failures, and build intelligent infrastructure.

Why AI in DevOps?

  • Detect issues before failure (Anomaly Detection)
  • Automatic root cause analysis
  • Predictive auto-scaling
  • Reduce alert noise
  • Self-healing systems
  • Deployment risk prediction
  • Security threat detection

Best AI Tools for DevOps

  • Dynatrace – Full-stack AIOps monitoring
  • Datadog – Cloud monitoring with AI
  • Splunk – AI-powered log analysis
  • Prometheus + Grafana – Open-source monitoring
  • OpenAI / AI Assistants – Automation & troubleshooting
  • Kubeflow – Machine Learning on Kubernetes
  • MLflow – ML lifecycle management

Future of DevOps
